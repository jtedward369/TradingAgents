### **Actionable Insights**
*   **Monitor Nasdaq-100 (QQQ) Correlation:** Since `TQQQ` is a leveraged version of the QQQ, all major technical breakouts or breakdowns in the Nasdaq will be magnified.
*   **Watch for Volatility Decay:** In sideways markets, `TQQQ` will lose value due to the daily rebalancing of the 3x leverage. Avoid heavy positions during period of low-volume, range-bound trading.
*   **Risk Management:** Given the speculative interest, expect "liquidity grabs" where price swings are sharp. Use tight stop-losses to protect against sudden reversals.
